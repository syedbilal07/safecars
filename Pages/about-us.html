4. HOW SAFE CARZ BEGAN.?

On April 5th, 2007, John Taylor & Mark Rubbins started Safe Carz Insurance Company. They wanted to provide vehicle owners with security and protection and they thought an insurance company was a good investment for a couple of peoples who were just getting started.
Safe Carz steady growth continued through the years, which helped fuel the decision to become a public company in 2015.
Our business philosophy was to approach auto insurance in an innovative way — like no other company had. From that mindset came the following industry standards and achievements:

  • In 2009, we introduced Immediate Response® claims service, available 24/7, to provide customers with personal service and support immediately after they reported a claim.

  • In 2010, Safe Carz was recognized as the largest seller of auto insurance through independent insurance agents.

  • In 2011, Safe Carz surpassed $2 billion in written premium.

  • In 2012, Safe Carz introduced a SSL (Secure Socket Layer) security to ensure that your insurance quotes are protected and safe just like us.
